A bit about myself...


My name is Max! I am a practicing glass artist and educator born in Tacoma, WA and currently residing in the San Juan islands. My primary focus is traditional Venitian style glassblowing, and for the last 8 years, I have dedicated most of my time to gaining as much working experience with the material as possbile. When I'm not in the studio, (or sleeping), I prefer to spend my time outside. I was born in Washington and grew up hiking, swimming, skiing and climbing and it still never ceases to amaze me that we have world-class access to so many activites within an hour or two here in WA. If I had to really narrow it down I would say my favorite things are:

  1. Reading!
  2. Anything glass-related!
  3. Some well-timed music
  4. FRESH AIR
  5. A hilarious joke!
